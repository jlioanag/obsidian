# Absolute time expression in Japanese
![[Pasted image 20230910141014.png]]
Use **に** when referring to absolute times. (Days of the week, exact times, months)...

This is the same as in English.
On Monday -> **げつようびに**
At 6 o'clock ->**ろくじに**
In July ->**しちがつに**
