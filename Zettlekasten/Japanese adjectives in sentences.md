# Japanese adjectives in sentences
The simplest form of an adjective sentence.
ペンが赤い。
(pen is-red)

Can change this to become a non-complete sentence:
赤いペンが。
(is-red pen) or "red pen" in English

Here's a full sentence:
赤いペンがちいさい。
"The red pen is small"
