# Three forms of Japanese sentences
![[Pasted image 20230909102749.png]]
> All forms have **AがB** but end with either:
		a **う**-ending verb, 
		noun + **だ**, 
		or with an adjective + **い**

> Example of I-Train: ペンが赤**い**
> 赤い means **is-red**
